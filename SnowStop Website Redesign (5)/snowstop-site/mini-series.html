Mini Series

Not every roof needs a full rail run. The Mini Series is a compact, low-profile guard for dormers, bump-outs, entryways, and smaller roof areas — the same TitanCore™ strength in a smaller footprint.

  • Ideal for dormers, porches, and short panel runs
  • Low-profile look that blends into the roof line
  • TitanCore™ polymer — strong and cold-rated
  • Available in Clear and Black finishes

TitanCore™ Clear stays clear

The Mini Series in TitanCore™ Clear is engineered to disappear on the roof and stay that way. While ordinary polycarbonate yellows, clouds, and grows brittle over time, TitanCore™ Clear holds its clarity and strength for 10+ years — so a guard installed for looks today still looks invisible a decade from now.

Pairs with the full line

Use the Mini Series alongside the Pro Series or Exposed Fastener Stand to cover transitions, valleys, and trouble spots on a larger roof. Same TitanCore™ material, same finish options, one consistent look.
