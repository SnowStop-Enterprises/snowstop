Built for the Canadian winter

Snow Stop is Canada's exclusive distributor of ArcticAnchor™ snow retention systems — engineered with TitanCore™ to keep snow and ice safely on metal roofs, from coast to coast.

Our story

Three decades in Canadian steel

Snow Stop was founded by two industry veterans with more than 30 years in Canadian steel importing — key suppliers to the market through three decades of building. That work gave them a rare, ground-level understanding of metal roofing: how panels are made, how they fail, and where the gaps in the market really are.

Snow retention was one of those gaps. So they set out to fix it — partnering with ArcticAnchor™ to develop a revolutionary snow-retention system built specifically for the demands of the Canadian winter. Today, Snow Stop holds the exclusive rights to distribute ArcticAnchor™ across all of Canada.

  • 30+ years of Canadian steel and metal-roofing expertise
  • Co-developed with ArcticAnchor™ — engineered, not improvised
  • Exclusive Canadian distribution, stocked and shipped coast to coast

Exclusively Canadian

The only source for ArcticAnchor™ in Canada

Through our partnership in developing the system, Snow Stop is the sole authorized distributor of ArcticAnchor™ snow retention across the country — with Canadian stock, Canadian pricing, and snow-load guidance for every region.

The material advantage

Engineered with TitanCore™

At the core of every ArcticAnchor™ guard is TitanCore™ — a proprietary polymer blend engineered for high tensile strength and resilience through extreme temperature swings, from punishing summer heat to −30°C cold. Where ordinary polycarbonate yellows and turns brittle, TitanCore™ holds.

That strategic partnership is why our product is, simply put, the most over-engineered and resilient snow-retention system on the market. This rail is being tested under 3,000 lb of direct force — far beyond any snow load it will ever face on a roof — and it holds.

What sets us apart
