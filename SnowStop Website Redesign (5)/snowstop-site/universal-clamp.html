Universal Clamp

The Universal Clamp grips the standing seam of your metal roof with hardened set screws — holding a continuous snow rail without drilling a single hole. No penetrations means no leaks and no compromise to your roof warranty.

  • Zero roof penetrations — clamps directly to the seam
  • Fits a wide range of standing-seam profiles
  • POM-tipped set screws grip tight without crushing the seam
  • Carries the same TitanCore™ rail rated to 3,000 lb

No penetrations

Set screws that grip — not puncture

Most clamps bite the panel with bare steel screws — dimpling the metal and starting the leaks that void your warranty. ArcticAnchor™ set screws are POM-tipped, so they lock onto the seam without ever going metal-on-metal. Click through to see how the seam stays sealed.

Bare steel screws dimple and pierce the panel — exactly where leaks and warranty claims begin.

304 stainless set screws arrive with preinstalled nylon thread-lock patches — they lock tight without liquid Loctite.

The white POM tip is the only thing touching your roof — rated to −50°F so it never scratches the finish or corrodes the panel.

Each screw is torqued to a precise spec — a firm, repeatable hold without crushing or deforming the seam.

The result: a continuous rail locked to the seam — with zero holes in your roof and your warranty intact.

One clamp, many seams

A single Universal Clamp fits a broad range of common standing-seam profiles, so you carry less inventory and spend less time matching parts to roofs. POM-tipped set screws bite firmly while protecting the panel's finish.
