Exposed Fastener · Weather-Tight

Exposed Fastener Stand

For through-fastened metal panels, the Exposed Fastener Stand mounts directly into the roof structure with a pre-applied butyl seal — locking the snow rail in place while keeping every fastener weather-tight.

  • Available in 1-hole and 2-hole configurations
  • Factory butyl strip seals each penetration
  • Clear version in development for a low-visibility look
  • Carries the TitanCore™ rail rated to 3,000 lb

Panel compatibility

Sealed against leaks

Every stand ships with a factory butyl strip on the base. As you torque it down, the butyl compresses into a continuous gasket around each fastener — so the very holes you create stay watertight for the life of the roof.

A clear version is coming

We're developing a clear, see-through version of the stand for a near-invisible look on the roof. Choose the 1-hole stand for standard loads or the 2-hole stand where extra pull-out strength is needed.
