Pro Series

When the snow gets serious, so should your retention. The Pro Series is our heavy-duty guard for high-snow regions and demanding exposed-fastener roofs — built to hold the heaviest loads winter can stack on a roof.

  • Maximum holding strength for deep-snow climates
  • TitanCore™ tested to 3,000 lb of load
  • Validated at −30°C — no brittle cracking
  • Available in Clear and Black finishes

TitanCore™ beats polycarbonate

Ordinary polycarbonate snow guards yellow, get brittle, and crack in extreme cold. TitanCore™ is engineered for Canadian winters — staying strong and clear where lesser plastics fail.

The result is documented performance: over 3× the holding strength of typical polycarbonate systems.

Stays clear, stays strong

The Clear finish keeps its appearance for 10+ years of UV exposure. Prefer it to disappear against a dark roof? Choose Black. Either way you get the same uncompromising hold.
