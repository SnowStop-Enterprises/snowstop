What is snow retention?

Stop the slide before it starts

On a metal roof, snow and ice slide off in one sudden, dangerous sheet. A snow retention system holds that snow on the roof so it melts and drains evenly — protecting gutters, vehicles, landscaping, and anyone standing below.

  • Prevents dangerous roof avalanches over doorways and walkways
  • Protects gutters, vents, and the roof panels themselves
  • Lightweight system — strong as steel, a fraction of the weight
  • One product line for standing seam and exposed-fastener roofs

Easy installation

Installed in 3 simple steps

No specialty crew, no roof penetrations on standing seam. Most runs go up fast with basic hand tools.

01

Attach the base

Clamp onto the standing seam — or fasten the stand to an exposed-fastener panel. No drilling into standing-seam roofs.

02

Set the rail

Slide the continuous rail through the base across the roof line, spaced to your snow load.

03

Tighten & done

Torque the set screws to spec. POM-tipped, pre-patched stainless screws lock tight without damaging the panel.
